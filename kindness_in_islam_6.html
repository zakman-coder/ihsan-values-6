The Pillars

Core Concepts of Ihsan ‚ú®

Rahma (Mercy)

The universal, all-encompassing mercy of Allah, which Muslims are mandated to reflect in their treatment of all creation‚Äîhumans, animals, and nature.

Adl (Justice)

Upholding fairness and righteousness. Ihsan complements justice; true excellence includes ensuring others receive their rights fully and fairly.

Qasd (Sincerity)

A pure intention (niyyah) that all actions are performed solely for the sake of Allah, ensuring the quality and integrity of the worship.
